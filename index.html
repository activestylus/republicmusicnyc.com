About

Republic Music is a unique new label and collective based in New York that connects producers, DJs and fans worldwide in the spirit of the underground.

From Brooklyn to the UK, Spain to Sydney and beyond, Republic aims to elevate the standard and get heads nodding in every hemisphere with highly advanced dance music that is grounded in the old school but looks to the next level.

The unifying principle of deep house – the ageless, timeless, all-encompassing sound of real house music – is the foundation of Republic's musical philosophy. However our roster of artists are inspired by and incorporate a rich kaleidoscope of contemporary sounds, including funk, garage, indie, jazz, Latin and electronica. Soul is the common element, and soul is found in all forms of music as long as heads put their heart in the mix. Not limited to one genre or tempo, Republic’s mission is to dish out the soul of the future. This diverse melting pot of grooves is the real “world music.” It’s a masterful fusion of acoustic and electronic; it’s dark as midnight and light as the promise of a new day; it’s just for a good time and it’ll save your life.

Republic stands for rebellion against the mainstream, against cynicism, against the same old way of doing things, against commercial noise. We walk a true path, keeping hope alive for a better time – a time when people realize they are already free, to be gathered under the sway of a universal soundtrack with power to soothe, uplift and heal.

Republic means unity in diversity and freedom in love. It also means kick-ass funky soul with zero bullshit. It’s more than a label – it’s a passion, a movement and a way of life. All with open minds are welcome. Come, join the movement, and take your first step into a larger musical world.

Neil Smallridge

facebooktwittersoundcloud

Hailing from Manchester, where he holds down a number of weekly DJ residencies, and a veteran of Ibiza's legendary Pacha, Neil Smallridge is well known for his deep house, garage and funk grooves which have kept discerning music heads rocking east and west for years.

This well-travelled selector's way with soulful sounds past, present and future inspired some globally attuned, musically diverse and - of course - smoking hot jams.

Neil currently resides at the Manchester hot spot 'The Blackdog Ballroom' where he plays a broad spectrum of house, disco (old/nu/indie), funk, hip hop and soul to a full house in an epic 8-hour set every week. With no shortage of flavors, sounds and inspiration, it is little wonder why he draws such huge and appreciative crowds.

Max Martinez

facebooksoundcloud

Max has been making some big noise on the East Coast scene with a string of steady-rocking remixes and originals for Maya Records, Aztec Tribalism, Selecta Recordings, the list is quickly growing for this rising star, whose sound people can't seem to get enough of.

Drawing inspiration from a rich musical background of hiphop, freestyle, house and funk, Max's releases are instant classics underscored by moody keys, floor stomping beats and dubbed-out soul, evoking the spirit of New York's deeply rooted dance culture.

Though he currently calls Pennsylvania home, Max's native Brooklyn roots are evident in his production style and flexibility. When he's not cranking out some of the sickest beats this side of the Atlantic, Max can be found tearing up dancefloors throughout the TriState area within a growing sphere of influence as more people discover his amazing talent.

Seph a.k.a. Dreamer's Dub

facebooksoundcloud

Seph has been a champion of the phenomenon known as House Music all his life: a fan, collector, historian and archivist since the music's earliest days, and now one of New York's most eloquent ambassadors of the real underground sound.

Appearing on several labels, from House Cafe Music to Maya Records, he produces and remixes under the names Dreamer's Dub and Valentin (with Maya's DJ Joeski), stacking up a collection of expressive originals and otherwordly remixes which feature Seph's penchant for emotive synths, smoking drumkits and sexy, sprawling basslines.

Inspired by the demand for this innovative sound, Seph continues to create, promote and tour from his hometown, spreading the message of House Music on many fronts. Whether rocking crowds worldwide, organizing memorable events or making beats in the studio, Seph is constantly working to redefine and shape what people think of real House Music.

Steven Garcia

facebooksoundcloud

Steven's mastery of dynamic production and evolved compositional sensibility are familiar to househeads on both sides of the Atlantic, thanks in part to standout tracks on compilations from Café del Mar and Turntables on the Hudson which have enjoyed global exposure.

The soundtrack Steven cultivates is driven by a lifetime passion for keyboards, percussion and record collecting. Though best known for his dancefloor offerings, Mr Garcia habitually produces a connoisseur's secret stash of funk, soul and electronica with tremendous dexterity.

This former Brooklynite currently lives in Spain, where he regularly wows audiences with sessions that serve as homages to the prolific pace of his muse. Both on and off the decks Steven establishes a stratospheric standard, pushing the boundaries of sonic possibility in a way that can only be described as artistic genius.

DJ Ino

facebooksoundcloud

Ino has contributed much to the electronic scene in the last decade. With efforts spanning several albums, collaborations and the launch of his own label House Cafe Music, he has put his hometown of La Coruña on the map by pioneering the forefront of House Music in Spain.

Among the impressive roster of artists and House legends which have appeared on his label, Ino's work stands out. His discography has left an indelible mark on a healthy collection of genres, mainly House, but also jazz, disco, and even a full-length HipHop album with a sound that is unmistakeably his own.

Ino currently holds events at 'La Terrazza', a space located in the five-star Hotel Finisterre that has managed to position itself as one of the most successful events in the city of La Coruña. He also runs Victoria Club from the hotel's massive and decadent ballroom, where a packed house gathers every month to hear the top-notch sound which has become the signature of this iberian visionary
